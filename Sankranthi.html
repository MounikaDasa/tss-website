Sankranthi Event

About Event:
Sankranthi is a major Telugu festival celebrated with great enthusiasm at Telugu Samskruthika Samiti, IIT Kanpur. The event is organized by the Samiti members and is open to all students and faculty members of the institute.
The Samiti members organize traditional cultural events followed by a feast of Telugu authentic bhojanam.
The event also features a number of other activities, such as games, competitions. The competitions include rangoli making, sports events, singing, dancing.
The Sankranthi celebrations at Telugu Samskruthika Samiti are a great opportunity for students and faculty members to learn about and experience Telugu culture. The event is also a great way to promote social interaction and camaraderie among the members of the institute.
