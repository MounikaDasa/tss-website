About Our TSS

Telugu Samskrutika Samiti ( TSS )

Telugu Samskruthika Samiti is a student organization started by IIT Kanpur students that promotes the Telugu language and culture on the campus.

Here are some specific things TSS will do:

1. Organize Telugu cultural events, such as festivals, performances, followed by authentic telugu bhojanam
2. Advocate for the needs of Telugu-speaking students on campus
3. Organize telugu movies screening on campus or off campus
4. Build community among Telugu-speaking students at the college
